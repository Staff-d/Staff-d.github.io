About

Hi everyone, I am Sebastian.

I started writing my first programs in my early teens and have been at it since. Driven by the desire to write my very own video games I got into shader programming and rendering. I later used this experience in my first job as an automation engineer, where I designed machine vision applications for autonomous industrial robots and quality control machines in a B2B project setting.

From there I switched to web- and cloud applications and worked for a medium-sized e-commerce company as a developer. Tired of working on predefined tickets I returned to the project business and combined the knowledge from my previous jobs to work on B2B IoT solutions.

At my current employer I lead a small team of developers and build IoT Applications on AWS.

I also have a Master's Degree in Computer Science from the Münster University of Applied Sciences.
